Your goal, as Decoder, is to crack the secret Code provided by the CodeMaster, in the least number of attempts.

The Code can be made up of any combination of the five colors.
The MasterCode can use two or more equal colors, in different positions.
You have to duplicate the exact colors and positions of the hidden Code.

The server ends when the Decoder guesses the secret Code.
Type /help to see all the commands available.

Follow the legend to enter your attempts and receive the feedback from the CodeMaster after every guess.
